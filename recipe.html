Grilled Ribeye Steak

Quantity Ingredients
Ribeye 12 oz., salt 1/2 tsp
pepper 1/2 tsp, cumin 1/2 tsp

Instructions

  1. Choose a well marbled Ribeye steak.
  2. Rest steak to room temperature.
  3. Heat grill.
  4. rub in salt pepper and cumin on both sides of
    room temperature ribeye steak.
  5. Place steak on hot grill.
  6. Cook for four minutes on each side.
  7. Press center of steak, it will rebound when ready.
  8. Rest steak for five minutes before serving.
